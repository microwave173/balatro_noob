# Balatro v1.3 Rulebook

Updated: 2026-04-26 13:30:00

## Summary Rules
- Skills and rules are guidance, not hard constraints; adapt flexibly to the actual game state, boss effect, money, hand cards, and current build.
- Use Joker abilities deliberately, but stay flexible when a Joker line conflicts with survival, boss safety, or a stronger scoring route.
- Before choosing a play or discard, estimate likely scores for the best available hand types and compare the chance of improving through discards against playing now or saving cards for a stronger hand.
- Do not use a purely greedy short-term policy. Consider out-of-round value such as scaling Jokers, Joker buffs that require specific hand types or actions, and the extra money from unused hands when the blind is already likely to be won.
- In the mid and late game, when money is high, consider selling weak or off-route Jokers and rerolling the shop to assemble synergistic Joker packages or find stronger power pieces.
- Scoring comes from Chips, Mult, and XMult multiplying together. When buying Jokers, identify the weakest scoring component and prioritize improving it; XMult is rare and powerful, but do not reroll so much that the economy collapses.

## Play Skills
- seed_play_rules_are_guidance_not_law: Treat learned skills and rules as advice rather than strict commands. Re-evaluate them against the current hand, boss, resources, and survival needs before acting.
- seed_play_estimate_score_and_improvement_odds: Before playing or discarding, estimate scores for plausible hand types and compare them with the blind target. If considering a discard, judge whether the probability of improving is worth the hand/discard resources.
- seed_play_use_jokers_flexibly: Try to trigger useful Joker abilities, scaling conditions, and buffs, but do not force a Joker line when a different play is safer, scores more, or avoids a boss counter.
- seed_play_consider_scaling_and_unused_hands: When the blind is likely to be won, consider actions that safely grow scaling Jokers or preserve unused hands for end-of-round money; by default, each unused hand is worth about $1.

## Shop Skills
- seed_shop_rules_are_guidance_not_law: Treat learned shop skills and rules as advice rather than strict commands. Adapt to actual money, boss threat, current Jokers, available shop items, and survival pressure.
- seed_shop_balance_chips_mult_xmult: Scoring is Chips x Mult x XMult. Identify whether the build lacks Chips, +Mult, or XMult, then prioritize shop buys that patch the weakest component while keeping enough money to survive.
- seed_shop_midlate_sell_and_reroll_for_synergy: In the mid and late game, especially with high money, sell obsolete or off-route Jokers and reroll for synergistic Joker packages, XMult, scaling, copy effects, or boss counters.
- seed_shop_value_scaling_jokers_without_tunnel_vision: Consider growth-oriented Jokers and buys that support their scaling, but do not overpay or force scaling lines when immediate survival or stronger scoring is required.
